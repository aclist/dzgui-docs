Development
===========

The project is open source under the GPLv3 license with the code being hosted on
`github<https://github.com/aclist/dztui>`. If you wish to contribute to
development, you will find further help in the
`CONTRIBUTING.md<https://github.com/aclist/dztui/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md>`
file.

All contributors are expected to act according to the projects
`code of conduct<https://github.com/aclist/dztui/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md>`,
and follow the projects standards and guidelines.
